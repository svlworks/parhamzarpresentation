The Growth & Virality Tactics

🚀

Going Viral: The Emotional & Educational Hook

The algorithm heavily favors extreme emotion and high-value "myth-busting". We will mine your existing footage for the most emotionally resonant clips (e.g., Intended Parents holding their baby for the first time) and controversial/high-value topics (e.g., Why surrogacy costs what it does). We will edit these specifically for high-retention to trigger algorithm pushes to the Explore page.

💬

Driving Engagement: Interactive Storytelling

Engagement is the metric that trains Instagram to show your content to your own followers. We will utilize IG Stories daily with interactive stickers: Weekly "Ask Parham" Q&A boxes, polls on common surrogacy misconceptions (e.g., "Fact or Fiction: Traditional vs. Gestational"), and slider reactions to success stories.

📈

Converting Followers: SEO & Profile Optimization

Instagram is a search engine. We will use keyword-rich captions (#LGBTQFamilyBuilding, #SurrogacyJourney, #EggDonationProcess) instead of generic tags. Every Reel and Carousel will end with a strong, tailored Call-To-Action (CTA) such as "Save this for your journey," "Share with someone who needs hope," or "Link in bio to book a consultation."
